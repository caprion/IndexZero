# M1 Hints

Five hints for the five hardest parts of M1. Each one explains the concept
and points you toward the right Python tools — but does not give you
copy-paste code.

Use them when you're genuinely stuck (tried something, it didn't work,
you don't know what to try next). Record which hints you used in your
decision log under "What went wrong first."

| Hint | When to open |
|---|---|
| `hint_1_accent_stripping.md` | Your accent test fails and you don't know what combining characters are |
| `hint_2_numeric_boundaries.md` | You can't figure out how to split "8GB" into "8" and "GB" |
| `hint_3_stemming_convergence.md` | "running" and "runs" produce different stems and you can't make them match |
| `hint_4_separator_strategy.md` | You're not sure whether to replace or remove hyphens, slashes, apostrophes |
| `hint_5_unicode_normalization.md` | You don't understand what NFKC does or why it matters |
